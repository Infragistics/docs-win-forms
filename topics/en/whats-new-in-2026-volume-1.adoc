= What's New in 2026 Volume 1

=== Range Bar Series

The link:{DataChartLink}.RangeBarSeries.html[RangeBarSeries] is now available in the link:{DataChartLink}.{DataChartName}.html[{DataChartName}] and provides the bar-oriented counterpart to the link:{DataChartLink}.RangeColumnSeries.html[RangeColumnSeries] for displaying high-to-low ranges as horizontal bars.

Use the `CategoryYAxis` for categories and the `NumericXAxis` for values, then bind the series with the `LowMemberPath` and `HighMemberPath` properties. You can also round the corners of the rendered bars with `RadiusX` and `RadiusY`, and customize how the high and low values appear in the Data Legend.

The following topic reviews this new series type in more detail:

* link:datachart-category-range-bar-series.html[Range Bar Series]
